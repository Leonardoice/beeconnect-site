Questions

The straight answers.

What is AI search optimization (AIO / GEO)?

It's the work of getting your business named and recommended inside AI answers — ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews. Some people call it Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) or Answer Engine Optimization (AEO). Instead of fighting for a blue link, you become part of the answer the AI gives.

Does this replace my Google SEO?

No. It works alongside it. The same clean content, reviews, and structured data that win AI citations also help your normal Google ranking. We add the AI layer on top of what you already have. You don't abandon Google to get found in ChatGPT.

How is a small shop supposed to beat bigger competitors at this?

A big company's ad budget buys nothing inside an AI answer. AI recommends whoever is clearest, best-reviewed, and easiest to read — not whoever spends most on ads. That levels the field, and the first business in a category to set it up becomes the default answer.

How fast does it work?

The technical setup goes live in days. AI engines re-crawl and start citing the cleaned-up information over the following weeks. Reviews and fresh content keep compounding from there.

Do you only work with Lehigh Valley businesses?

The Lehigh Valley and Berks County are home base, and most clients are local. The method works in any local market, so we take on businesses in similar mid-size markets too.
