How RepKit Works
1
Customer completes a booking
Via BookKit or manual entry in RepKit admin
2
RepKit sends a review request
SMS or email with your branded review link, sent automatically after a configurable delay
3
Customer rates + picks a platform
They see your branded review page, rate their experience, and get directed to Google, Yelp, or Facebook
4
You manage + respond from the hub
All reviews in one place. Response templates. Negative alerts. Analytics.
